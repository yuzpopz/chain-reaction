How to Play

Objective

Be the last player with orbs on the board. Capture enemy cells by triggering chain reactions that overflow into neighbouring squares.

Taking a Turn

  • Click any empty cell or a cell already owned by you to place an orb.
  • You cannot place on an opponent's cell.
  • Players alternate turns until only one remains.

Critical Mass & Explosions

Every cell has a critical mass equal to the number of its neighbours. When a cell's orb count reaches that limit — it explodes, sending one orb to each neighbour and converting them to your colour.

Chain Reactions

Explosions can trigger further explosions in neighbouring cells — potentially cascading across the whole board in a single turn. Plan your moves to set off massive chain reactions!

Elimination & Victory

  • After every player has taken at least one turn, a player with no orbs is eliminated.
  • The last player with orbs on the board wins.
